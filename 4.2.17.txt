The note for this final says: "Some may follow /-aj/ in CPR. The
'Mongol Rimes' follows [-jaj]." Most words gave PR /-jej/, which may be
considered an earlier form of the Modern Mandarin /-je/. The initial /ŋ/ is
not distinct in PR.
