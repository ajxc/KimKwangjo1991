	This final /-jew/ must have evolved into /-jaw/ in contemporary actual
speech. The *Hongmu chông'un yôkhun* and the *Sasông t`onghae*, as a whole,
give PR /-jaw/ for this final in their notes. The following list exhausts
all the variant readings indivudlally recorded.
